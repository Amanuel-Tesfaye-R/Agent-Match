Features
Tracks Every Meal

Breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks — log calories and macros from any meal. Start on one device, pick up on another.

Grows With You

Persistent nutrition memory and auto-adjusted targets — it learns your habits and never forgets what worked for your goals.

Scheduled Check-Ins

Natural language reminders for meals, water intake, and weekly weigh-ins — running automatically through your preferred channel.

Meal Planning AI

Isolated meal plan generators with their own recipe databases and macro calculators for zero-guesswork nutrition pipelines.

Real Ingredient Data

Five data sources — USDA, OpenFoodFacts, branded products, custom recipes, and barcode scanning — with portion normalization.

Full Food Database

Web search for any dish, ingredient scanning, portion estimation via vision, recipe import, and multi-model macro breakdown.
